I <3 Design.

My name is Emily, and I am a visual designer by education, user experience designer by trade, and systems thinker by nature, with a passion for applying my skills in all of the above areas. In 2007, I graduated cum laude with a BFA in Visual Communication Design from the University of Washington. Though based in Seattle, I've worked with talented people from London to India to Atlanta in order to turn great designs into great products and experiences. I have worked with a range of nationally known clients, including Microsoft, The Coca-Cola Company, Kraft Foods, and the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation.

I am currently a senior user experience designer at Microsoft, responsible for the design language of the MSN app suite across all platforms, including the web. I love all things design related outside of the office, too - my latest adventure has been working towards my professional continuing education certificate in HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design for the web.
